General Drilling & Completion Procedures

20” Casing While Drilling Level 2

1. M/U Weatherford CWD Spear to Top Drive.

2. M/U 24” CWD Bit with Pup joint to 20” 133# X56M SL-BOSS casing

17-1/2” Hole Section Drilling

1. M/U 17-1/2” Rotary BHA + LWD, RIH BHA, drill shoe track, and perform LOT to 14.3 ppg.

2. Continue drilling until 2200m TVDDF. POOH BHA.

12-1/4” Hole Section Drilling

M/U 12-1/4” rotary BHA + LWD, RIH BHA, drill shoe track, and perform LOT to 16 ppg.

Continue drilling until 3157m TVDDF (40m from most likely TOC). POOH BHA.

8-1/2” Hole Section Drilling (Carbonate)

1. N/D overshot, install RCD, N/U overshot, pressure test.

2. M/U 8-1/2” motor BHA + LWD, M/U seal assembly running tool, RIH BHA, drill shoe track & perform LOT to 17.3 ppg.

Well Testing

1. M/U 7” lower assembly (7” liner hanger + Well Annular Barrier assembly + Guide shoe).

2. In case of total losses, RIH 7” liner assembly in top kill mode. Once RIH with DP, install seal assembly, close RCD on 5-1/2” DP and activate PMCD.

3. In case of partial losses, cure losses via LCM, flow check.

4. RIH 7” liner assembly conventionally once the well is static.

5. Set 7” liner 80m above 9-5/8” shoe. Retrieve liner hanger running tool. Pressure test above 7” liner packer.

6. RIH 9-5/8” scraper BHA, tag top of liner and scrape packer setting depth @ 3000m TVDDF.

7. Circulated well with clean up pill and later circulate with overbalance Safebrine.

Casing Program & Design Criteria

Hole Size Casing Specification TD Criteria/Casing seat Justification
17-1/2”
  • 20” 133# X56M SL-BOSS
  • 20” 133# X56M JV-LW
  • 20” is designed to withstand total load from Riser, BOP, 18-3/4” unitized WH, 13-3/8” & 9-5/8” csg with the assumption of subsequent load from 7”” etc. would be shared/supported by the 9-5/8” cement which will not jeopardize the integrity of the 20” conductor.
  • 13-3/8” shoe depth satisfies 50 bbls kick tolerance based on the most likely MW against high case pore pressure.
  • To isolate from unconsolidated formation.
